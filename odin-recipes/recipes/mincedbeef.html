Minced Beef and Rice Recipe

Description

Place a pan on the stove and set to medium heat. Add some olive oil to the pan. Once, pan is heated add the minced beef and set a timer for 10 minutes. As the mince is cooking, break it apart with a spatula or wooden spoon. Add plenty of salt, pepper and paprika while the mince is cooking and stir. Once the timer is at 2 minutes, add the rice to the pan and stir it together. After the timer is finished, wait for the mince to cool down for a minute, then serve

Ingredients

  • Minced beef
  • Rice
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Paprika

Recipe Steps

  1. Place pan on stove and set to medium heat
  2. Add some olive oil
  3. Once pan is hot add minced beef and set timer for 10 mins
  4. While mince is cooking, break it apart and add spices
  5. After 8 mins have passed, add the rice and stir
  6. Once the timer is done wait a minute then serve
